This exploratory study uses conjoint analysis to quantify the relative importance of five packaging attributes which are deemed significant to oral care products in the UK. Conjoint analysis is a commonly used method to explore consumer preferences in marketing. A large body of conjoint analysis research focuses on fast moving consumer goods (FMCG), particularly packaged foods. Comparatively, very little is known about consumer preferences towards less hedonic, non-food FMCG product categories such as oral care. The oral care market, although mature, continues to grow and is highly competitive.
